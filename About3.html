How it works


Blook is a book swapping platform. At Blook you can
join a community where you can explore a vast range
of books. Blook allows you to pick a book to
request or send to a fellow Blooker.

Established in 2019, we wanted to make countless
books accessible through this online community
of users all of over the city.

Blook makes book swapping easy. Simply select
the profile of the book. Here you can engage
in reviews, online forum or request the book.
Alternatively enter the book you have to give away
and we will notify you once it has been requested.
